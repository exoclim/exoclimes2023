Registration

Exoclimes VII has capacity for 200 participants, 50 of which can also be accommodated on the ExoSLAM summer school. There is a two stage process, first an application or pre-registration (including estimated costs to inform your decision), followed by registration and booking both through links on this page. If you are applying to present your work at Exoclimes VII, note that the abstract will be used in the selection process, but it can be amended later at the registration stage (if selected).
Exoclimes VII: July 7 to 11, 2025     |     ExoSLAM: July 3 to 5, 2025

Applications closed on January 24, 2025

Indicative Costs
